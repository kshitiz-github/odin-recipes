Panner roll

Description

Paneer roll is a popular Indian street food featuring marinated paneer cubes sautéed with onions and bell peppers, rolled in flatbreads or parathas. The paneer is marinated in a mixture of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala for at least 30 minutes to infuse it with rich flavors. Once sautéed until tender, the paneer mixture is placed on the flatbreads, drizzled with mint chutney or tamarind sauce, and rolled up for a quick and flavorful snack or meal that captures the essence of Indian cuisine.

  • bell peppers
